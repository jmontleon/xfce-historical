Chuck Mead, from Moongroup consulting, Inc, has provided XFce with a Mailing List and a new mirror site www.xfce.org.
          Subscribe now to XFce's mailing list to get the latest infos and share you feelings and requirements about XFce with other XFce's users.

          1) Subscribing

          You can get on the list by sending an email to xfce-list-request@xfce.org with the word "subscribe" (no quotes) in the subject line.

          2) Unsubscribing

          To unsubscribe send an email to xfce-list-request@xfce.org with "unsubscribe" without the quotes in the subject line.

          3) Mailhelp archives

          Mail archives can be viewed from http://www.xfce.org/Listarch/threads.html
