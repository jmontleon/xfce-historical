Welcome to XFCE, a free graphical interface for the X Window System, based on XForms.  

    The XFCE project was started mainly because I needed a simple, light and efficient toolbar for my Linux System. 

    There are a lot of real good toolbars and interfaces for Unix based systems, but most are too heavy, or too expensive, or even both ! 

    I wanted something easy to use and configure, stable, fast, and, at last but not least, visually appealing... 

    ...So here comes XFCE  2 !
